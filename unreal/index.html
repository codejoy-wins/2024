Shock Rifle FPS Demo

Designed & developed in Unreal Engine 5.5 by Maxwell Jann

Gameplay Features

  • Chain-reactive shock core explosions that can launch the player and trigger other cores
  • Destructible generators as objectives to win
  • Rotating AI enemies with configurable aggression and health
  • Custom radial damage logic using Blueprint interfaces
  • Recharging Shields, and Respawn System with limited Lives
  • Moving Platforms and Teleporters
  • Audio and Visual delay effect on shock combos that resemble thunder and lightning

Technology Used

  • Unreal Engine 5.5
  • Blueprint visual scripting
  • Niagara for visual effects
  • Custom AI movement and targeting logic
  • Physics-based projectile behavior and overlap detection

About the Developer

Built by Maxwell Jann – systems designer, gameplay programmer, and indie creator focused on expressive mechanics and clean, replayable loops.
